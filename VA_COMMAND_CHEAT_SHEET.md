# BeeSmartVA Team Bot — VA Cheat Sheet

## Daily flow

1. Check your tasks:
   - `/tasks`
2. Log your hours as you work:
   - `/hours today 2.5 LinkedIn outreach`
3. Track outreach:
   - `/connection Jane LinkedIn Founder ACME`
   - `/followdone Jane`
   - `/replied Jane`
4. Submit drafts:
   - `/draft linkedin Here is the draft copy`
5. At the end of the week:
   - `/myweek`
   - `/submit hours`
